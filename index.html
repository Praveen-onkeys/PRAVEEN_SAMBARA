About the House

Current Ramu Gari House is not just a building — it is a living symbol of dedication, service, and family values in Yeditha. This house stood as a silent witness to decades of hard work, respect, and contribution to the village.

The house is deeply associated with our grandfather, who devoted his entire life to serving people with honesty and skill. Through this home, generations learned the importance of discipline, humility, and helping others without expectation.

Our Grandfather

Name: Ramu Garu

Profession: Government Certified Electrical Contractor

Born: May 5, 1955

Passed Away: January 12, 2019

He served the village for more than 45 years, providing reliable electrical services and guidance to countless families. Known for his integrity, kindness, and dedication, he earned respect not just as a professional but as a truly good human being.
